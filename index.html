About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and AI risk. IDR collaborates with academic partners and global organisations to prepare professionals for high-consequence technology environments.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between research, education, and industry practice through training programs, innovation projects and advisory services.

Service Pillars

Academy

Bootcamps and training programs for students and professionals in cyber risk, governance and digital security.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing digital risk models, research in AI governance, and future cyber risk frameworks.

Advisory Services

Supporting organisations with frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2 compliance.
